Poetry

Lyeanne, a name that flows so sweet, A soul so rare, a joy to meet. With eyes that shine like stars above, And laughter like a song of love.

    Lyeanne, a name that flows so sweet, A soul so rare, a joy to meet. With eyes that shine like stars above, And laughter like a song of love. Lyeanne, a spirit bright and kind, A heart that's warm and true to find. With grace and beauty all her own, A light that shines, a heart full-grown.

      Lyeanne, a name that will not fade, A treasure found, a friend well-made. For in her presence, we feel the love, A gift from heaven, sent from above.
